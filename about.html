about.

I'm a web designer trying to show the charm of the vast web.

Since 2020, I've enjoyed turning complex problems into simple, beautiful, and intuitive designs. When I'm not developing, you'll find me daydreaming of a better tomorrow, one pixel at a time.

Backstory

I started this journey when I took a couple basic coding classes in college. When the world shut down in 2020, I found a real interest in bringing ideas to real actual life applications.
Self-teaching how to code is one of the challenging things I've had to do, but I truly see the impact Namib can have with helping bring small business and entreprenuers' dreams come to life.
I started this company because I love finding diamonds in the rough. The Namib desert is the oldest desert in the world and home to the Skeleton Coast, forbidden lands, and every Safari animal you've ever dreamed of.
Too many stories to cast a small net over. I embed that in my design to share our life stories and goals through the work.
