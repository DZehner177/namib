Background + Artistic Philosophy

Working in the field of Architectural Photography for the past five years, I aim to see the beauty in modernity. Beauty is present everywhere, but I find it especially in concrete jungles and industrial behemoths. I don't capture buildings; I capture space, lines, contrasts, and forms as they all come together into one great structure.
